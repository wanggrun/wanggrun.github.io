Short Bio

Guangrun Wang is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Engineering Science at University of Oxford, working with Prof. Philip H. S. Torr. Before that, he was a PhD candidate in the HCP Lab of Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Liang Lin. He also worked as a visiting scholar in the MMLab of The Chinese University of Hong Kong, mentored by Prof. Ping Luo and Prof. Xiaogang Wang.

His research interest is machine learning.

His GPA is 95, ranking 1st in his class. He receives the 2018 Pattern Recognition Best Paper Award. He was awarded as an "Outstanding Student of Guangdong Province". He ranked 1st in 2015 Graduate Innovation Funding of The High Performance Computing Collaborative Innovation Center(only 7 graduate students were awarded in China that year). He won several worldwide competition medals such as Kaggle data mining gold (out of about 1000 teams)/silver/bronze medals. He ranked 1st in PASCAL VOC segmentation leaderboard for months. His team ranked 1st/2nd place out of 2322 teams in FashionAI Global Challenge. He won two National Scholarships. Has was awarded SYSU Excerpts of Dissertation (Top 5%) and SYSU Outstanding Graduate (Top 5%). He was awaded Excellent intern in DMAI (the first employee to have this honor). He has two ESI Hightly Cited Paper. He was awarded as an outstanding reviewer of ICLR 2021. He was awarded as an outstanding reviewer of NeurIPS 2021. He was awarded as an Outstanding Reviewer of ICCV 2021. He was awarded as a Distinguished Senior Program Committee member (only 18 members, announced at the IJCAI-21 Opening Ceremony). He has been fortunate enough to serve as a reviewer for the papers of Turing Prize winners several times. He is in the list of Top Chinese Rising Stars in Artificial Intelligence. He is awarded Wu Wen-Jun Best Doctoral Dissertation Award (only nine people were awarded in China in 2021).

He serves as a Distinguished Senior Program Committee member (only 18 members, announced at the IJCAI-21 Opening Ceremony) and a Senior PC for IJCAI, Program Committee Board of IJCAI (PCB for 2022, 2023, and 2024), outstanding reviewer for NeurIPS, ICLR and ICCV, conference reviewer for ICML, NeurIPS, ACL, CVPR, AAAI, ECCV, EMNLP, EACL, IJCNLP, NAACL, AACL, WACV, ACCV, PRCV, journal reviewer for ISPRS, T-PAMI, T-NNLS, T-IP, T-CSVT, PR, IEEE Access, ISPL, JVCI, IEEE/CAA JAS, IPM, IF, DT.

He has obtained double Bachelor Degrees, including Engineering and Business.
